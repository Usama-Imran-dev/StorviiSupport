What is Storvii?

Storvii is a storytelling platform where users can share personal stories, join virtual story rooms, and connect through voice chats and instant messaging.

How can I contact customer support?

Go to the Help & Support section in the app and click Contact Us button below to send a query or report an issue.

How do I create an account?

Open the app, click Sign Up and follow the steps to register using your email or social media account.

How do I reset my password?

Click Forgot Password on the login screen, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

How do I edit my profile?

Go to the Profile section, click the Edit button, and make the desired changes. Save your updates when done.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note that this action is irreversible.

How can I create a story room?

Navigate to the Create Room option, set a theme or topic, and choose whether the room is public or private.

Can I change the privacy of my story room after creation?

Yes, navigate to the room settings, and you can switch between public and private options anytime.

How do I invite someone to my private story room?

In the room settings, select Invite Participants, and share the invitation link or directly add users by their usernames.

How do I delete a story room?

Go to the room settings, select Delete Room, and confirm. Note that this action is irreversible.

Can I host a live event on Storvii?

Yes, you can schedule and host live storytelling sessions by selecting the Live Event option in the room settings.

How do I follow another user?

Visit the user's profile and click the Follow button to stay updated on their activities and stories.

How can I provide feedback on a story?

At the end of a story, use the Rate and Review option to provide feedback and help improve the storytelling experience.

How do notifications work?

Notifications alert you about activities such as new followers, scheduled story sessions, and messages. You can customize these in the notification settings.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you encounter inappropriate content, click on the Report option and select the issue you wish to report.
